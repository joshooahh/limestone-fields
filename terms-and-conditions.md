## Property Rules

To protect the experience for all guests and the integrity of the property, the following rules apply:

**Quiet Hours:** 10:00 PM – 8:00 AM. Please be respectful of neighbors and fellow guests.

**Smoking:** Smoking and vaping are not permitted inside any structure on the property. Designated outdoor areas may be used. Violation will result in a deep cleaning fee of no less than $500.

**Pets:** Pets are welcome with prior written approval and a refundable pet deposit. Unauthorized pets may result in forfeiture of the security deposit.

**Fires:** Open fires are only permitted in designated fire pits. Never leave fires unattended. During burn bans issued by Leon County or the State of Texas, all outdoor fires are strictly prohibited.

**Lake & Water Safety:** Swimming, kayaking, and watercraft use are at guests' own risk. Limestone Fields does not provide lifeguards. Guests assume full responsibility for their safety on and near the water.

**Firearms:** Texas law permits licensed carry. All guests are expected to handle firearms safely and responsibly. Reckless use of firearms on the property is grounds for immediate removal.

**Recreational Substances:** Consumption of recreational substances not legal under Texas law is not permitted on the property.
